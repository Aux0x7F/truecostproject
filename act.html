Bring tips, time, or money.

There are three ways to get involved with the True Cost Project. Pick the one that fits where you are.

Volunteer

Our volunteers do the kind of work that makes accountability possible. They read public records and pull out what matters. They help build research files on the institutions we are investigating. They create content, support campaigns on the ground, and keep this organization running between the headlines. If you have time and you want to put it toward something that will outlast a news cycle, we will find a place for you.

Volunteer

Send a tip

If you work inside an institution that receives public money for animal research, animal agriculture, or any related program, and there is something the public should know, we want to hear from you. Tips can be submitted anonymously through a secure form. We protect our sources.

Submit a tip

Donate

The True Cost Project is independent. We do not take money from industry, from government, or from any source that would compromise the work. Every dollar goes into investigations, legal pressure, and public-facing campaigns. If you want this kind of accountability work to exist and to grow, this is how it gets funded.
